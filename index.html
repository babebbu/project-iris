About Iris

Who We Are

Intelligent Refuelling Information System (IRIS), is the first aviation refuelling management system that has been elaborately developed by integrated the management system with the safety information system for aircraft refuelling services.
As a world leading in aviation refuelling services, IRIS has succeeded in the development and designed for best experience of look and feel with user friendly for the best safety in operation. Besides, IRIS will significantly increase the performance of service especially the on-time performance and safety performance.
IRIS is highly flexible to support complex business process of the aviation refuelling services around the world.

Vision

Be a leader in aviation refuelling system

We provide the best platform to enable real-time integration across the complete global fuel supply chain incorporating all the activities and parties involved. Our mobile computing expertise and innovative design enables the removal of paper and manual entry through fully understanding the needs of each stakeholder, our platform is designed to help realise significant efficiencies and optimisation across your organisation.
